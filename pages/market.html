Target Verticals

Priority #1

Healthcare Practices

TAM: $9.74B / SAM: $2.92B. Medical practices, dental offices, and specialty clinics with complex billing, insurance reconciliation, and compliance needs.

Pain Points: Insurance billing complexity, compliance requirements, fragmented systems between clinical and financial operations.
Priority #2

Construction and Trades

TAM: $1.49B / SAM: $447M. About 3.7M construction businesses in the US, most with fewer than 50 employees using QuickBooks and spreadsheets.

Pain Points: Job costing, subcontractor payments, payroll complexity, compliance, and zero real-time visibility into project profitability.
Priority #3

Restaurants

TAM: $2B / SAM: $600M. Strong payroll and location-level reporting pain, but higher tool-switching risk due to POS integrations.

Pain Points: Multi-location financial reporting, payroll for tipped employees, food cost tracking, and compliance across jurisdictions.

Hiring Growth

Cash Crunch

Margin compression, late invoices, or cash flow surprises. Owners realize they need better visibility.
